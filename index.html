T & C

Terms and Conditions

  • Please read the above list of companies that EEC has sent requests for internship opportunities. Kindly remember that EEC is only responsible for finding internships not the job opportunities to students.
  • All the companies do not inform their internships opportunities on same day. Therefore we will be constantly updating the website about vacancies and the deadlines. When applying to the vacancies please be kind enough to accept the vacancy if you are selected to it. If you have been selected to more than 1 company within 3 days period you can choose between companies. Otherwise you will be wasting a valuable chance to one of your batch mate. It is really difficult to find internship opportunities these days, therefore please be responsible for the selection. Do not wildly submit your CV to each and every company in the website.
  • In the given link for CV submissions we have displayed the responded companies and the no of vacancies available for each company. Few companies has requested specific no of CVs, in that case we have no other choice than follow first come first basis manner. Before opening that sort of link we will notify the date and time in the group. Therefore please apply that sort of opportunity only if you really want it.
  • At the end of each deadline we will show the list of applicants for that particular company. The reason for showing it is to give you a sense of competition you have and to take the sense of probability you are getting selected to it. (Remember do not consider only about GPA, in an interview you are evaluated for communications skills too.)
  • If you wish to find internship or job opportunity from external contacts inform about it and make sure not to waste a vacancy from limited opportunities.
  • Finally it is up to you upgrading your internship opportunity into a job opportunity at the end of the training period. Good luck!
